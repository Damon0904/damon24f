Random Thoughts

The Silence Between Waves

November 15, 2023

There's something profound in the space between waves—that momentary stillness when the ocean seems to catch its breath. I find myself seeking those pauses in my own life, the quiet moments between activities where everything settles and clarity emerges.

We often focus on the waves themselves—the big moments, the actions, the visible movements. But the magic might actually be in those silent intervals where everything resets.

Morning Light Through Different Windows

October 28, 2023

I've been noticing how the morning light changes depending on which window I view it through in my home. The kitchen window shows it filtered through oak leaves, my bedroom window frames it rising over distant hills, and the bathroom window (of all places) catches the most brilliant golden reflections.

It makes me wonder—how many other things in life appear completely different based on which "window" we choose to view them through?

The Weight of a Stone

October 10, 2023

I picked up a smooth stone at the beach today—just an ordinary gray stone that fit perfectly in my palm. As I held it, I thought about how this simple object contained entire geological eras, countless waves that had shaped it, and now, for this brief moment, my attention.

We carry so much through life—not just physical objects but memories, ideas, relationships. Some feel light as feathers, others heavy as stones. But even the heaviest things we carry have been shaped by time and experience into something that fits perfectly in the contours of who we are.
